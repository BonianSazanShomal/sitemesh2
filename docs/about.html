Some History

SiteMesh was originally written about 5 years ago, after Joe Walnes downloaded the first sun servlet engine and wrote it using servlet chains (before filters).

He had been using it internally for about 2 years and by the time it was opensourced it was quite mature. Over the years, the basic design remained the same; content was intercepted, parsed, a decorator mapper was used to find a decorator and they were merged together.

There were 2 main leaps that helped make it succesful - the rewrite from servlet-chain to servlet-filter when the first draft of the Servlet 2.3 API came out about 3 years ago and Victor Salaman rewriting the parser to make it about 12 times faster.
