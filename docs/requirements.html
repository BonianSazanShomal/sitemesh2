SiteMesh is a Servlet Filter and therefore requires a container that conforms to the Servlet 2.3 specification.

As SiteMesh is written in pure Java, it is platform independent.

SiteMesh has been successfully tested on the following Servlet containers:

  • Orion 1.5.4 and up
  • Jakarta Tomcat 4.0 and 4.1
  • Resin 2.1.11
  • Oracle OC4J 2
  • WebLogic 7.0 SP2 and 8.1
  • Jetty 4

The only other requirement of SiteMesh is JAXP (with a parser implementation). This should be supplied by your servlet engine.
